<h2 class="text-3xl font-bold text-slate-900 mb-6">The Challenge: Rising Water, Late Warnings</h2>
<p class="text-slate-600 text-lg mb-6 leading-relaxed">
Flooding remains one of the most destructive natural hazards worldwide, especially in disaster-prone regions like the Philippines. Communities in low-lying areas often lack accessible, real-time data to prepare for rapid water rises.
</p>
<p class="text-slate-600 text-lg mb-8 leading-relaxed">
<strong>WATCHEYE</strong> bridges this gap. By integrating ultrasonic sensors and GSM communication, we provide an affordable, community-centered solution that informs residents immediately when flood thresholds are crossed.
</p>
